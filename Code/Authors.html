Peters Morgan

Before going into the commercial sector and providing tech assistance, site design, and programming upgrades, I spent my youth teaching High School Mathematics and Computer Studies. Eventually, my passion for gaming drew me in, and I began to apply my programming talents to the gaming world. For many individuals, gaming is intrinsically enjoyable, and the emergence of games as an acceptable form of social networking in the last generation has been amazing. I feel we are only at the beginning of our gaming future, and I am excited to see where it leads us. My first Gamemaker app was recently launched for iOS and Android. Slyddar Studios' Twos! is the name of the game.

Felix Alvaro

My name is Felix, and I'd want to introduce myself. I am a Web Designer, Entrepreneur, Mentor, and Author that is enthusiastic about what I do. First and foremost, I want to commend you on your efforts to educate yourself. I appreciate that not everyone has the time, patience, or financial resources to invest in classes, attend college, or learn how to code, program, or establish an internet company by trial and error, but I am confident that there are many individuals out there like you who want to master these abilities. People, in my opinion, should have access to high-quality information and instructional content that is appropriate for them at a cheap cost and does not take up a large amount of their time and energy.
